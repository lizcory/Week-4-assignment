Covid deaths in the US (2/08/21)

Across the country, COVID-19 deaths are declining. though there is some variation across states and counties. Scroll down below for a closer look at three states: New York, Massachusetts, and Colorado.

New York
New York saw high death rates this week, particularly in Kings, Queens, and Bronx counties.
Massachusetts
Massachusetts likewise had relatively death rates, with peaks in Middlesex, Worcester, and Essex counties.
Colorado
Colorado, in contrast, had low death rates, overall, though Denver, El Paso, and Jefferson counties brought the state count up.
